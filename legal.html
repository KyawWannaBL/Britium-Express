Legal & Policies

Transparent terms for a secure logistics partnership.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy Liability & Insurance Refund Policy

1. Terms of Service

Welcome to Britium Express. By using our services, you agree to the following terms. We reserve the right to update these policies at any time.

1.1 Prohibited Items

Britium Express does not transport the following items:

  • Explosives, firearms, or ammunition.
  • Illegal drugs or narcotics.
  • Live animals (without prior special arrangement).
  • Highly flammable liquids or gases.
1.2 Right of Inspection

Britium Express reserves the right to open and inspect a shipment without notice for safety, security, customs, or other regulatory reasons.

2. Privacy Policy

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2.1 Data Collection

We collect information necessary to process your shipment, including sender and receiver names, addresses, and phone numbers.

2.2 Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:

  • Facilitating delivery and tracking.
  • Customs clearance documentation.
  • Communicating service updates.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Liability & Insurance

3.1 Standard Liability

Britium Express exercises utmost care in handling your shipments. However, our liability for loss or damage is limited to 5 times the shipping fee or the declared value, whichever is lower, unless additional insurance is purchased.

3.2 Force Majeure

We are not liable for delays or loss caused by events beyond our control, including but not limited to:

  • Natural disasters (floods, earthquakes).
  • Civil unrest or government actions.
  • Traffic accidents caused by third parties.

4. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Cancellations: You may cancel a booking before the rider has picked up the package for a full refund. Once picked up, shipping fees are non-refundable.

Service Failures: If a time-guaranteed shipment (Priority Express) is delayed due to our fault, we will refund the difference between the Express and Standard rate.
